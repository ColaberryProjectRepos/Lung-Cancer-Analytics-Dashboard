Executive Summary:



Problems and issues during the project:

1. Data Transformation Issues: The original data was comprised of aggregate values that directly affects the analysis and dynamics of my dashboard. After extensive research I found out the Sex column has an extra row labeled as Males and Females, which is the sum of Males and females that have died or diagnosed with lung cancer. Filtering out those values before transforming the data resulted in accessing the correct values.

2. Finding the right DAX calculation: It took me a while to figure out cancer related rate calculation should be based on 100,000 population size instead of 1000 or 100.

3. Choosing the right visual for my dashboard: Adding a scale-bar legend to my map visual and removing the scrolling feature from my title bar was a bit challenging.
