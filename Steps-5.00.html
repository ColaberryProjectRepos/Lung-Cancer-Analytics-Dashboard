Suggestions:

1: More fund for lung cancer research is needed to find preventive measures, better screening techniques and advanced treatments for cancer patients. Although the lung cancer incident rates increased by 6.21% between 2003 and 2013, the mortality rate decreased by 10.3% due to advanced drugs and therapies, reduced smoking rates and early screening. 
2: Affordable health care for all will encourage everyone to do a regular checkup and increases the chance that cancer cases would get caught at the early stage to receive effective and lifesaving treatments. 
3: Maintaining healthy lifestyle including diet, exercise and avoiding smoking habits might lower the chances of getting lung cancer.
